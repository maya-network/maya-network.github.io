the Yarn Network

The Yarn Network connects women and minority entrepreneurs from around the world with backers mentors and investors who understand the power of ideas coming from diverse backgrounds in business. We find early stage startups and small businesses with strong teams and compelling stories, vet them for depth of industry experience, passion, and teamwork, and share their stories with expert audiences like seed funds, angel investors, and venture capitalists as well as with people like you, who want to learn from innovators across the world. Our goal is to create a new startup ecosystem that redistributes investment away from tech hubs like Silicon Valley and into areas of the world without strong entrepreneurship ecosystems yet full of innovators.
